About the maker

Made one stitch at a time 🧶

Crochet Keychains is a small, hand-run studio in Pakistan. Every charm here is crocheted slowly and carefully — chosen yarn, safe stitched-on eyes and sturdy hardware — so it can be carried, squished and loved for a long time.

Because each one is made by hand, no two are exactly the same, and that's the whole point. Have a colour, character or idea in mind? Send it over and we'll crochet something just for you. Browse the gallery above, or follow along on Instagram for new designs.
